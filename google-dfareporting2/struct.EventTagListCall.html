Retrieves a list of event tags, possibly filtered.

A builder for the list method supported by a eventTag resource. It is not used directly, but through a EventTagMethods.

// You can configure optional parameters by calling the respective setters at will, and

             .sort_field("et")

             .add_ids("kasd")
             .add_event_tag_types("kasd")
             .enabled(true)
             .definitions_only(true)
             .campaign_id("nonumy")
             .advertiser_id("sanctus")
             .ad_id("voluptua.")
